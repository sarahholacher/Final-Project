Why Pocahontas Still Matters

Disney’s Pocahontas (1995) is more than just a movie—it's the most unforgettable, heart-wrenching love story Disney's ever told. Here's what makes it unforgettable:

  1. The Love Story
    • Pocahontas and John Smith each learn about the others culture and come to really appreciate it
    • Unlike most Disney love stories, their physical chemistry guides their connection
    • Their love story does not end happily ever after, instead they must say goodbye
  2. Powerful Music
    • "Colors of the Wind" won an Academy Award
    • "Savages" has deeper ties to relevant societal issues
    • "If I Never Knew You" symbolizes the impact they left on each other and makes me cry every time
